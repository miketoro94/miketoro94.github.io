About Me

I have always been into technology and building. I began to take an interest in gaming and technology when I was in middle school.

At first, I just spent hours and hours playing different games, becoming the best at it I might add. Of course, my parents were concerned at the amount of time I spent playing that my dad decided to show me beyond just playing games.

He showed me how it was that my computer worked and its different parts, he showed me how to build my own and make it the way I wanted it to be.

I learned the different parts that needed to be changed and fixed to make my PC its very best. This woke the curiosity in me, and it made me want to understand how websites worked and functioned, that’s when I began to learn about coding.

As a self-learner I learned many things but never enough, with the help of a program I entered I have been able to pursue this further and with these tools hope to someday get hired by an important company.
